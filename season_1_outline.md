# Mailstorm - Season 1: "The Mandate"

**Theme**: *Bureaucracy vs. Innovation* (The Soul of the Service vs. The Algorithm)
**Legacy Tracker**: This document tracks the permanent changes to the world state.

---

## The Core Conflict
*   **The Protagonists (D.O.L)**: Underfunded, cursed, but protected by **The Union Contract**. They use "Gut Feeling" and "Spite".
*   **The Antagonists (Speedy-Go)**: Infinite budget, soulless, backed by **The Shareholders** (Eldritch). They use "Optimization" and "Data Leeches".

---

## Episode List

### Ep 1: The New Hire (The Orientation)
*   **Lore Introduced**: The Federal Building (The Dungeon), The Phantom Tray (Anomaly).
*   **Plot**: Heather joins the D.O.L. as a CCA (Initiate). Chuck tries to explain the "Safety Brief". Stan is cursed with a *Phantom Tray* that refills instantly.
*   **The Monster**: **The Phantom Tray** (Object Class: Thaumiel).
*   **Legacy Change**: Heather gains "The Scanner" (Level 1). Stan admits he cares (slightly).

### Ep 2: The Algorithm's Gaze (The Invasion)
*   **Lore Introduced**: Speedy-Go, The Sprawl, Data Leeches.
*   **Plot**: Speedy-Go opens a "Hub" across the street. They release **Data Leeches** that drain the D.O.L. scanners. Chuck tries to "Optimize" the floor and accidentally summons a **Minor Audit Demon**.
*   **The Monster**: **Data Leeches** (Technoparasites).
*   **Legacy Change**: The D.O.L. declares "Digital War". Ace (Speedy-Go) is introduced as Stan's rival.

### Ep 3: The Route (The Gauntlet)
*   **Lore Introduced**: Route Cryptids, The Green Tag Bag, Animal Interference.
*   **Plot**: Stan takes Heather on "The Widowmaker" route. They face **Porch Pirates** (Goblins) and a **Mailbox Mimic**. Stan teaches Heather the "Animal Interference" spell to bypass a Level 50 Karen.
*   **The Monster**: **Mailbox Mimic** and **The Karen**.
*   **Legacy Change**: Heather survives her first route. She unlocks "The Strut" (Passive Buff).

### Ep 4: The Audit (Mid-Season Climax)
*   **Lore Introduced**: Audit Demons, The Board, The Red Tape.
*   **Plot**: Because Chuck filed a form in *Blue Ink* instead of *Black*, The Board sends a **Level 8 Audit Demon**. The office must go silent ("The Quiet" Protocol) to survive.
*   **The Monster**: **Audit Demon** (Sound-Hunter).
*   **Legacy Change**: Chuck is demoted (then promoted again). The Office Joy Level drops to -10%.

### Ep 5: Dead Letter (The Heist)
*   **Lore Introduced**: The Dead Letter Office, Paper Golems, Benjamin Franklin.
*   **Plot**: Stan loses a "Sacred Letter" (a love letter from 1945). The team must descend into **The Basement** to retrieve it from the **Paper Golems**. They meet The Ghost of the Founder.
*   **The Monster**: **Paper Golems** (Tank Class).
*   **Legacy Change**: The team gains the **Arrow Key** (Skeleton Key).

### Ep 6: The Strike (The Paladins)
*   **Lore Introduced**: The Union, Paladin Oaths, Scabs (Drones).
*   **Plot**: Speedy-Go tries to replace the carriers with Drones. Barb (Union Rep) initiates a "Localized Strike" which freezes time in the building. A standoff between Paladins and Drones.
*   **The Monster**: **Speedy-Go Drone Swarm**.
*   **Legacy Change**: The Drones are defeated by EMP (Microwave + Stapler). Union Rep Barb reveals her Power Level.

### Ep 7: Black Friday (The War - Part 1)
*   **Lore Introduced**: The Shareholders, The Sprawl.
*   **Plot**: The busiest day of the year. The Shareholders demand "Infinite Growth". Speedy-Go attempts a hostile takeover by literally winching the Federal Building into The Sprawl.
*   **The Monster**: **The Shareholders** (Eldritch Presence).
*   **Legacy Change**: The Federal Building is unmoored from reality.

### Ep 8: The Mandate (Season Finale)
*   **Lore Introduced**: The PMG (Lich King), The Reality Shift.
*   **Plot**: To save the D.O.L., Stan invokes **The Mandate** (The Constitutional Duty to Deliver). He challenges Ace to a "Final Delivery" through **Route 666**.
*   **Climax**: Stan delivers the package. The PMG grants a single boon: "Stability".
*   **Legacy Change**: The D.O.L. survives another year. Heather becomes a Regular. Stan smiles (once).

---

## World State (End of Season 1)
*   **D.O.L. Status**: Surviving, but monitored by The Board.
*   **Speedy-Go Status**: Retreated to The Sprawl, plotting revenge.
*   **Characters**: Team is bonded. Use of "Magic" (Bureaucracy) is now standard procedure.
