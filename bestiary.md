# The Bestiary (Classified Field Guide)

**Security Level**: Paladin Eyes Only
**Survival Rating**: Extreme
**Property of**: The Department of Logistics (Archives Division)

---

## 1. Audit Demons (The Sound-Hunters)
*   **Scientific Name**: *Bureaucraticus Auditorus*
*   **Containment Class**: **Euclid** (Requires Active Suppression)
*   **Danger Level**: **High (8/10)**
*   **Habitat**: Ceiling tiles of the Federal Building, or any room with "Unauthorized Joy".
*   **Biology**:
    *   Formed from **Red Tape** (solidified bureaucracy).
    *   **Echolocation**: They are blind but can hear a giggle from three floors away.
    *   **Diet**: They feed on *Morale* and *Overtime*.
*   **Handling Protocols**:
    *   **Silence**: Do not speak. Do not laugh. Do not hum.
    *   **Malicious Compliance**: If confronted, follow their instructions literally until they freeze in a logic loop.
*   **Attacks**:
    *   **The Write-Up**: A physical scratch that drains 10 WP (Willpower) per second.
    *   **The Quiet**: Mutes all sound within 20 feet.
*   **Loot Drop**:
    *   **Golden Pen**: +5 Authority.
    *   **Rejected Form**: Can be used to banish lesser demons.

## 2. Paper Golems (The Junk Mail Titans)
*   **Scientific Name**: *Constructus Spam*
*   **Containment Class**: **Keter** (Aggressive Accumulation)
*   **Danger Level**: **Medium (5/10)** (High in swarms)
*   **Habitat**: The Sorting Floor (The Pit) and The Dead Letter Office.
*   **Known Incidents**:
    *   **The Great Flyer Flood of '08**: A Golem made of grocery ads breached containment and buried a Supervisor in coupons.
*   **Biology**:
    *   **Composition**: 40% Grocery Ads, 30% Political Flyers, 30% "Current Resident" Scams.
    *   **Variants**:
        *   **The Flyer (Light)**: Fast, causes *Paper Cut* (bleed damage).
        *   **The Catalyst (Heavy)**: Made of phone books. Immune to physical damage. Must be burned.
*   **Handling Protocols**:
    *   **Incineration**: Fire is the only permanent solution.
    *   **Return to Sender**: Stamping this on their forehead causes critical Holy Damage.

## 3. The Automobile Gremlins (The LLV Saboteurs)
*   **Scientific Name**: *Mechanicus Malignus*
*   **Containment Class**: **Safe** (If bribed)
*   **Danger Level**: **Low (3/10)**
*   **Habitat**: The engine block of every Long Life Vehicle (LLV).
*   **Biology**:
    *   Small, soot-covered goblins.
    *   **Symbiosis**: They keep the 40-year-old trucks running, but crave *Chaos*.
*   **Handling Protocols**:
    *   **The Bribe**: Leave a 10mm socket or 87-octane fuel on the dashboard.
    *   **The Warning**: If the engine hums a sea shanty, get out immediately. It is about to implode.
*   **Attacks**:
    *   **Spontaneous Combustion**: Lights the engine on fire to stay warm.
    *   **The Stall**: Cuts fuel when pursued by a dog.

## 4. Data Leeches (The Technoparasites)
*   **Scientific Name**: *Digitalus Vampiricus*
*   **Containment Class**: **Euclid** (Digital Contagion)
*   **Danger Level**: **Variable (depends on Wi-Fi strength)**
*   **Habitat**: Speedy-Go Terminals and The Sprawl.
*   **Biology**:
    *   Neon-blue lampreys existing in the AR layer.
    *   **Parasitism**: They latch onto Scanners (MDDs) to drain battery and harvest *Private Data*.
*   **Handling Protocols**:
    *   **Faraday Isolation**: Wrap device (or head) in aluminum foil.
    *   **The Union Contract**: Acts as a firewall.
*   **Loot Drop**:
    *   **Corrupted Data Shard**: Can be sold on the black market.

## 5. The Route Cryptids (Street Legends)
*   **The Porch Pirate (Goblinus Klepto)**
    *   **Ability**: *Shadow Step*. Turns invisible when a package touches the porch.
    *   **Counter**: Bait packages filled with glitter or bees.
*   **The Mailbox Mimic**
    *   **Identification**: Looks like a blue box. Teeth inside the slot.
    *   **Test**: Breathe on the metal. If it fogs, it's alive.
*   **The Karen (Customerus Entitlus)**
    *   **Rank**: Level 50 Boss Monster.
    *   **Attack**: "Can I speak to your Manager?" (Psychic Scream, Stun 10s).
    *   **Defense**: "I am the Manager" (Lie). **Warning**: Enrages the beast.

## 6. The Anomalies (Object Class: Thaumiel/Appollyon)
*   **The Infinite Loop**: A cul-de-sac that generates houses faster than you can deliver.
    *   **Escape**: Turn left three times, then drive through a hedge.
*   **The Phantom Tray**: A tray of mail that refills instantly.
    *   **Cause**: Punishment for Hubris (thinking you can finish early).
