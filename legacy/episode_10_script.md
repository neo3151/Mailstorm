# Episode 10: "The Initiate and the Stationary Event" (Season 2 Premiere)

**GENRE:** Dark Workplace Comedy / Shonen Action 
**TONE:** High-octane, exhausted, highly stylized.

---

## Storyboard (Visual Beat Sheet)
*   **Act 1: The Trap.** Extreme close-up of CCA Kip Baxter trapped in a non-Euclidean "Pivot Reality-Marble". The MDD scanner triggers a "Stationary Event," summoning Supervisor Chuck's projection, who casts a *Catch-22 Geass* on Kip. Kip panics, misusing an *Orange Dog Ward* and summoning an Invisible Fencebeast.
*   **Act 2: The Strike.** Stan, a jaded Table 1 Veteran, vaporizes the beast with a pure kinetic strike using a single size-64 rubber band, shattering the Geass.
*   **Act 3: The Heretic.** A rogue speedster—a "Runner" Heretic—phases through the neighborhood, leaving a thick, toxic wake of "Time-Debt" that instantly rots the grass. The DOIS Oracle algorithm detects this anomaly.
*   **Act 4: Penalty Overtime.** The sky turns bruised purple. *V-Time Hunter Drones* deploy because the carriers are nearing double-pay. Stan and Kip escape in Stan’s rusted LLV mail truck. Stan weaponizes the LLV Dashboard Fan, blasting 130-degree hellfire out the window to melt a drone.
*   **Act 5: The 8 & Skate.** Cornered at the edge of the Reality-Marble, Kip survives by drawing blood and casting the *1571 Absolution* shield. As the clock strikes exactly 08:00:00, Stan activates the forbidden *8 & Skate Teleportation Jutsu*, warping them to safety. 

---

## Script

**[SCENE START: ACT I]**

**EXT. 61ST DISTRICT SUBURBS - DAY**

The sun beats down. It’s 98 degrees. Cicadas scream in the trees, providing a maddening soundtrack to the endless rows of identical, perfectly manicured lawns.

They are too identical. If you look down the street, the same blue house with the same white picket fence repeats endlessly into the heat-haze. Space is looping.

KIP BAXTER (20s) stumbles up a driveway. He wears an oversized postal pith helmet. His Green Tag Bag is distended, packed with suffocating Redplums. 

He coughs. A tiny puff of gray dust escapes his lips. The *Table 2 Bloodline Curse* is actively burning his marrow to generate the meager energy he needs to carry the bag.

He reaches a mailbox. Shoves a stack of mail inside. Slams it shut. 

He stops, resting his hands on his knees. Just taking a breath.

A digital timer floats in the air beside him: **04... 05... 06 seconds.**

**KIP**
(Wheezing)
Okay. Just... break the loop. Just one more.

**SFX: A DEAFENING, BASS-BOOSTED BEEP.**

The sound comes from the MDD scanner on Kip's hip. It glows piercing red, casting a bloody hue over the manicured lawn.

The cicadas stop. The warm sunlight snaps into a sickly, flickering fluorescent green. 

**SFX: BASS DROP.**

The gravity around Kip triples. He is slammed to his knees. A localized "Stationary Event."

A pixelated shadow stretches across the grass, elongating unnaturally to form CHUCK, the District Supervisor. The shadow isn't attached to anyone. 

**CHUCK'S DIGITAL SHADOW**
*Stationary Event recorded, Baxter. Failure to Follow Instructions!*

The words carry the weight of the *Catch-22 Geass*. Red, jagged rings of energy snap around Kip's ribcage. His eyes widen in agony as his internal organs begin to boil.

**KIP**
I—I just needed water—my bag is—

**CHUCK**
*Logic is insubordination. The Algorithm demands blood.*

Desperate, Kip pulls out a laminated, glowing Orange Dog Warning Card. He slams his bloody palm down onto the concrete driveway, trying to cast a protective ward. 

He misses the property line by an inch. The card flashes a sickening, inverted purple.

The air distorts. Blue static electricity arcs between the grass blades. From the static, THE INVISIBLE FENCEBEAST materializes—a towering hound made entirely of jagged electrical lines. It lunges to consume Kip's remaining willpower.

**SFX: THWIP—BOOM!**

A shockwave rattles the windows of every house on the block. The blast of wind knocks Kip backward. 

The Fencebeast is gone. In its place: dissipating sparks. Embedded into the brick of the house, smoking with unadulterated kinetic energy, is a single size-64 rubber band.

A wave of golden, overflowing mana washes over the lawn. The moment the light touches Kip, the red rings of the Catch-22 Geass shatter. Kip pulls in massive gulps of air.

He turns his head. Leaning casually against a rusted blue collection box is STAN (50s). 

Stan radiates a towering, effortless golden aura—the pure, unfiltered power of a Table 1 Veteran. He is casually flipping through a thick U-Line catalog, completely unfazed.

**STAN**
(Deadpan, not looking up)
You’re blocking the box, kid. 

**KIP**
Did... did you just kill a voltage-demon with office supplies?

Stan closes the catalog. His sheer apathy pushes Chuck's green fluorescent projection away entirely.

**STAN**
It’s a size 64. Tensile strength of a steel cable if you know how to snap it. Get up. The pivot isn't going to walk itself.


**[SCENE START: ACT II & III]**

Kip struggles to his feet, gripping his Green Tag bag.

**KIP**
I don't even know where I am. Every house looks the same. 

**STAN**
It’s a Pivot Reality-Marble. Management folded the zoning laws to fit an extra hour of work onto your slip. 
(Stan squints, sniffing the air)
Smells like burned rubber and bad math. Brace yourself, kid.

Suddenly, the ambient temperature drops twenty degrees. The grass at Kip’s feet instantly turns gray and rots.

A blur of motion streaks down the center of the street. It’s moving so fast it phases straight through a parked minivan. It’s a carrier, sprinting with dead eyes.

**KIP**
What was that?!

**STAN**
(Spits on the ground)
A "Runner" Heretic. Tapping forbidden velocity magicks to skip his breaks and go home early. He just left a trail of toxic Time-Debt across the whole sector. 

Above them, the sky turns the color of a bruised plum. An alarm sirens in the distance. 
A massive, booming robotic voice echoes from the clouds.

**DOIS ORACLE VOCAL (V.O.)**
*ANOMALY DETECTED. TIME-DEBT ACCUMULATED. PENALTY OVERTIME PROTOCOL: ENGAGED.*

From the purple clouds, sleek, metallic, floating mechanical eyeballs descend. They have glowing barcode-scanners for pupils.

**STAN**
V-Time Hunter Drones. Management protects the budget with lethal force. Run!

Stan grabs Kip by the collar and throws him toward the curb, right into the open sliding door of an ancient, battered 1987 LLV Mail Truck. 
"The Chariot of Fire."


**[SCENE START: ACT IV & V]**

**EXT/INT. STAN'S LLV - CONTINUOUS**

Stan slams the gas. The LLV’s engine screams, sounding like a dying washing machine. They careen wildly through the looping suburban streets.

Two V-Time Drones flank the vehicle. One drone charges a red laser in its eye.

**STAN**
Take the wheel!

Stan physically shoves Kip into the driver's seat. Stan leans out the passenger side window. He reaches under the dashboard and rips the legendary *LLV Dashboard Fan* from its mounting brackets.

Stan points the tiny metal fan directly at the Drone. 

**STAN**
Eat 130-degree engine block air, you tin-can parasite!

He flips the switch. The fan roars like a jet turbine. A physical beam of distorted, super-heated hellfire blasts out, instantly melting the drone's circuitry. It crashes into a mailbox, exploding.

**KIP**
(Struggling with the steering wheel)
The wheel isn't turning! The reality-marble is overriding the axle!

The LLV hits a magical barrier disguised as a cul-de-sac curb and flips outward, crashing onto its side.

Kip and Stan crawl out of the windshield. 

They are surrounded. Three V-Time Drones hover above them. Chuck's pixelated Shadow reforms, projecting from the drones.

**CHUCK'S DIGITAL SHADOW**
*Checkmate, Carriers. You are 45 seconds from Penalty Overtime. You are terminated.*

Stan pats his pockets. Empty.

**STAN**
I'm out of 64s. 
(He looks at Kip)
Hope you paid attention in orientation, kid.

Kip’s hands are shaking. He looks at his MDD. The clock reads **07:59:45**. 

Kip reaches into his shirt pocket and pulls out a blank piece of paper. The mythical *PS-Form 1571*. The Report of Undelivered Mail.

Kip bites his own thumb until it bleeds. He smears his blood across the signature line.

**KIP**
I AM WORKING SAFELY!

Kip slams the bloodied form onto the pavement. 

The *1571 Absolution* spell detonates. A massive dome of blinding white kinetic energy erupts from the paper. The Drones shriek as their sensors burn out. Chuck's projection shatters into static.

The shield holds, giving them a pure pocket of silence. 

Stan looks at his watch. **07:59:58.**

**STAN**
(Smirking)
Not bad, Initiate.

Stan grabs Kip by the collar again. Stan forms a complex series of hand-seals with his free hand. 

**07:59:59.**

**STAN**
Eight. And. Skate.

**08:00:00.**

**SFX: SILENT IMPLOSION.**

Space folds inward. Kip and Stan vanish into thin air, bypassing physical reality entirely.


**EXT. 61ST DISTRICT POST OFFICE PARKING LOT - CONTINUOUS**

A flicker of light. Kip and Stan slam onto the asphalt next to Stan's civilian car. 

Kip is hyperventilating, laying on his back, staring up at the normal, un-glitched evening sky.

Stan dusts off his compression leggings. He unlocks his car door, tosses his satchel in the trunk. He pulls a single, pristine size-64 rubber band from his pocket and flicks it onto Kip’s chest.

**STAN**
Don't be late tomorrow. We stir the UBBM Sludge.

Stan gets in his car and drives away. Kip lays on the asphalt, clutching the rubber band, a slow, exhausted smile spreading across his face.

**[SCENE END]**
