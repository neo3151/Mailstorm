# Mailstorm Factions & Hierarchy

## The Government (The Public Sector)

### 1. The Department of Logistics (D.O.L.)
*AKA: "The Post", "The Service", "The Snail"*
-   **Status**: A Constitutionally Mandated Agency.
-   **Mission**: "To Bind the Nation Together" (Article 1, Section 8).
-   **Vibe**: Ancient, Underfunded, Eternal. The walls are painted "Bureaucratic Beige".
-   **The Motto**: "It Will Get There. Eventually."
-   **The Strength**: **Authority**. They have the legal right to enter any building, cross any border, and commandeer any elevator.
-   **The Weakness**: **Budget**. Equipment is from the 80s. Staff is underpaid.

#### The Hierarchy
1.  **The Postmaster General (The Lich King)**:
    -   A mythical figure bound to the Constitution. Hasn't been seen in decades.
2.  **The Inspectors (Audit/Internal Affairs)**:
    -   Federal Agents with badges and guns. They hunt "Mail Crimes".
3.  **Middle Management (Chuck)**:
    -   Political appointees or people who failed the Civil Service Exam twice.
4.  **The Civil Servants (Stan/Heather)**:
    -   Impossible to fire, impossible to motivate.

## The Private Sector (The Rivals)

### 1. Speedy-Go (The "Disruptors")
-   **Concept**: A private tech company trying to "Privatize the Mail".
-   **Legal Status**: Government Contractors (they want the D.O.L.'s contract).
-   **Aesthetic**: Neon Green, Carbon Fiber, Subscription Models.
-   **Motto**: "Democracy is slow. We are fast."

## Ancient Relics (Government Property)

### 1. The Founding Quill
-   **Myth**: The pen used to sign the original Postal Clause.
-   **Power**: Can rewrite local reality (zoning laws).

### 2. The Budgetary Axe
-   **Effect**: Instantly cuts any object (or department) in half.
