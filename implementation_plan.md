## 📜 THE UNION CONTRACT (The Paladin Oaths)
*The rules that bind the Abyss. Violating these generates "Grievance Mana".*

- **The Article 8 Vow (The 12/60 Barrier):**
    - **Ability:** A kinetic armor that hardens the longer the carrier works. At exactly 11:59:59, the carrier becomes invulnerable but starts losing 10 HP/sec.
- **The Weingarten Rite (Summoning the Steward):**
    - **Ability:** A defensive summon. If a Supervisor begins an "Investigation," the carrier can manifest a **Holographic Steward** who nullifies all psychic/logic attacks until the investigation ends.
- **The Article 16 Shield (Just Cause):**
    - **Ability:** A "Truth Zone." Any attack from Management that isn't backed by "Direct Documentation" is reflected back at the attacker with double force.

## ⚔️ THE HIERARCHY (RPG Classes & Ranks)

### Level 1-10: The CCA (City Carrier Assistant)
- **Role:** Glass Cannon / Trash Clearer.
- **Weapon:** The **Green Tag Bag** (Infinitely heavy, used for blunt force).
- **Limit Break:** **"Permanent Conversion"** - A desperate gamble to gain Career status, doubling all stats but risking a "Life-Debt" to the Algorithm.

### Level 11-40: The Regular (Career Carrier)
- **Role:** Balanced Battle Unit.
- **Perk:** **The Uniform Allowance.** Magical equipment that regenerates over time.
- **Aura:** **"The Route Knowledge"** - A passive field that reveals hidden traps and "Nixie" phantoms within a 5-mile radius.

### Level 41-60: The Shop Steward (Branch Paladin)
- **Role:** High-Tier Support / Executioner.
- **Signature Move:** **"Step 2 Grievance"** - A reality-warping strike that erases the legal existence of a minor Supervisor within a local domain.

## 🏢 THE WORKPLACE (The Dungeon Layers)

### Layer 1: The Sorting Floor (Sensory Hell)
- **Mechanic:** Procedurally generated rows of **Hot-Cases**. Carriers must sort "Mind-Flayer Mail" (letters that scream) or take psychic damage.
- **Boss:** **The 204b Parasite** (A low-level crawler trying to evolve into a Supervisor).

### Layer 2: The LLV Graveyard (The Rust Domain)
- **Mechanic:** A wasteland of decaying vehicles. Carriers must "Tame" a rusted LLV to survive the **130°F Kiln-Storms**.
- **Environmental Hazard:** **"The Fluid Leak"** - Slippery, toxic mana pools that cause "Stationary Events" if stepped in.

### Layer 3: The Supervisor's Podium (The Throne of the Catch-22)
- **Mechanic:** The gravity here is 20G. You cannot speak without a "3996" ritual.
- **Boss:** **Chuck "The Overseer"** (Host for the Algorithm's localized multi-eye).
