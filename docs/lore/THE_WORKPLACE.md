# WORKPLACE MAPPING: The Corporate Dungeon
**SETTING:** Branch 61 / The Abyss.
**MECHANIC:** Layered Descent (Increasing 10G+ Gravity and Logic-Loops).

---

## 📍 LAYER 1: THE SORTING FLOOR (The Pit)
*The Sensory Labyrinth.*
- **Topography:** Thousands of 7-foot-tall vertical cases (The Maze).
- **Environmental Hazard:** **"The Heavy Parcel Rain."** Buffering steel belts overhead that may drop 40lb "Tape Golems" at any time.
- **Mana Node:** **"The Hot-Case."** A centralized altar where the most urgent "Mind-Flayer Mail" is kept.
- **Boss:** **The 204b Parasite (Ace Nakamura).**

---

## 📍 LAYER 2: THE LLV GRAVEYARD (The Rust Domain)
*The Transitional Void.*
- **Topography:** A 5-acre cracked asphalt wasteland hidden behind a 12-foot chain-link fence (The Cage).
- **Environmental Hazard:** **"The Kiln-Storm."** Temperatures reach 130°F. Carriers without **Heat-Stroke Berserk** take 5 HP/sec.
- **Mana Node:** **"The Gas-Point."** A ritual site where carriers must offer "Mileage Data" to the Algorithm in exchange for fuel.
- **Boss:** **The Mailbox Mimic (King CBU).**

---

## 📍 LAYER 3: THE SUPERVISOR'S PODIUM (The Ziggurat)
*The Seat of the Catch-22.*
- **Topography:** A raised, carpeted platform that defies gravity.
- **Environmental Hazard:** **"The Silence Zone."** All sound is absorbed. Communication is only possible through **3996 Blood Pacts**.
- **Mana Node:** **"The Multi-Eye Terminal."** A screen that displays the real-time "Breadcrumb" souls of every carrier in the city.
- **The Throne:** A reclining ergonomic chair that has fused with the Algorithm’s core.
- **Apex Boss:** **Chuck "The Overseer" Daniels.**

---

## 📜 THE DUNGEON MASTER\'S RULES
1.  **Stationary Events:** Any carrier who stops moving for more than 5 seconds in any layer triggers a localized gravity crush (10G).
2.  **The Pivot Rule:** Any exit from a layer requires a "Pivot Key" issued by a Supervisor.
3.  **The Union Shield:** Areas within 3 feet of a **Shop Steward** are "Safe Zones" where architecture cannot warp.
