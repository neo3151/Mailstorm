# THE FORBIDDEN FORMS: Administrative Sorcery & Bureaucratic Warfare
**THEME:** Every PS Form is a spell. Every grievance is a weapon. The paperwork IS the magic system.
**CORE MECHANIC:** DOCUMENTATION (The carrier's true power source).

---

## I. THE CARRIER'S GRIMOIRE (Offensive Forms)

### PS FORM 3996 — "The Time Pact"
- **Real Function:** Request for auxiliary assistance or overtime authorization.
- **Abyssal Spell:** A formal challenge to the Algorithm's time-prediction model. The carrier inscribes their projected return time in blood-ink. If the Supervisor denies the request and the carrier returns late, the denied 3996 becomes a **"Spite Shard"** — evidence for a grievance that can drain up to $500 from the Supervisor's quarterly Performance Budget.
- **Tactical Use:** A denied 3996 is MORE powerful than an approved one. Veterans intentionally request them knowing they will be denied, stockpiling Spite Shards.

### PS FORM 1571 — "The Absolution Scroll"
- **Real Function:** Report of undeliverable mail (dog obstruction, unsafe conditions, no access).
- **Abyssal Spell:** A ward that severs the carrier's obligation to a cursed address. By filing a 1571, the carrier can abandon a delivery point for 24 hours without triggering a "Failure to Deliver" curse. The Algorithm HATES this form — it represents a loophole in its control.

### PS FORM 3971 — "The Escape Rite"
- **Real Function:** Leave request (sick leave, annual leave, FMLA).
- **Abyssal Spell:** A teleportation scroll. When a carrier submits a 3971 for sick leave, they vanish from the Algorithm's tracking grid for 8 hours. However, the Algorithm remembers. Three consecutive 3971s trigger a "Fitness for Duty" investigation — a Level 60 Inquisition where the carrier must prove they are physically worthy of the burden.

### PS FORM 1767 — "The Hazard Declaration"
- **Real Function:** Report of unsafe working conditions.
- **Abyssal Spell:** A nuclear option. Filing a 1767 summons an **OSHA Avatar** — an impartial, Level 90 Arbiter from outside the Postal Dimension. The Avatar can shut down an entire Layer of the dungeon until the hazard is resolved. Supervisors FEAR this form.

## II. THE STEWARD'S ARSENAL (Grievance Spells)

### STEP 1 — "The Informal Inquiry"
- **Mechanic:** A verbal challenge. The Steward confronts the Supervisor directly. No paperwork. It is a test of will — Bureau-Kinesis vs. Catch-22 Geass. If the Steward wins, the violation is corrected immediately. If the Supervisor holds, the conflict escalates.

### STEP 2 — "The Formal Grievance"
- **Mechanic:** Written documentation submitted to the Postmaster. This is where the "Spite Shards" (denied 3996s, ignored 1767s) are assembled into a formal weapon. The grievance generates a **"Remedy Request"** — a demand for back-pay, restored leave, or even the removal of the offending Supervisor.
- **Power:** A Step 2 Grievance has the power to physically banish a Floor Supervisor from the building for up to 14 days.

### STEP 3 — "The Arbitration Rite"
- **Mechanic:** An external ritual. An **Arbitrator** (a neutral Level 100 entity from the National Agreement Dimension) is summoned. Both sides present evidence. The Arbitrator's ruling is ABSOLUTE and BINDING. It rewrites the local reality of the Post Office.
- **Legendary Use:** In 1976, an Arbitration ruling retroactively granted 6 months of back-pay to every carrier in a city, bankrupting the local Management's mana reserves.

## III. THE MANAGEMENT COUNTER-SPELLS

### THE PDI (Pre-Disciplinary Interview) — "The Inquisition"
- **Real Function:** A formal meeting where a Supervisor presents evidence of a violation and the carrier must respond.
- **Abyssal Form:** A psychic interrogation conducted in the Silence Zone. The Supervisor reads charges from a glowing screen. The carrier's only defense is to invoke the **Weingarten Rite** (Article 17) and summon a Steward. Without a Steward, the carrier's words are weaponized against them.

### THE 7-DAY SUSPENSION — "The Exile Curse"
- **Mechanic:** A formal punishment that removes the carrier from the Abyss for 7 days. While exiled, the carrier's route is "pillaged" by CCAs — their case setup is destroyed, their customers receive wrong mail, their reputation (Route Knowledge buff) degrades.

### THE 14-DAY SUSPENSION / REMOVAL — "The Kill-Shot"
- **Mechanic:** The most powerful Management attack. Attempted removal from the Service. However, this spell has a fatal flaw — it MUST survive the Grievance Engine. In practice, 85% of removals are overturned at Step 3 Arbitration, because Management rarely has sufficient "Just Cause" evidence. The Union's shield is nearly impenetrable.
