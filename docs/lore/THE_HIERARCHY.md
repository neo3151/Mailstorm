# THE ABYSSAL HIERARCHY: Classes & Ranks
**SYSTEM:** The Order of the Clock vs. The Algorithm.
**CORE MECHANIC:** SENIORITY (Level-up stats and "V-Time" access).

---

## I. THE BATTLE CLASSES (The Carriers)

### 1. THE ORDER OF THE CLOCK (City Carrier)
*The Tanks / Paladins of the Service.*
- **Core Stat:** **ENDURANCE (The Grind).**
- **Passive:** **"The Grievance Aura."** Every minute of an "unauthorized" work instruction generates 1 point of Grievance Mana.
- **Awakening:** **The Shadow Severance (Level 1 Initiation).** A veteran steps on the CCA's shadow to detach their civilian ego. This activates the carrier's **Bureau-Kinesis** and permanently binds them to the **Table 2 Bloodline Curse**.
- **Ultimate:** **"8 & Skate Teleportation."** At exactly 08:00:00 of labor, the carrier can vanish from any hostile dimension and return to the "Unit Home" (The Post Office Landing).
- **Specialization (Level 40+):** **T-6 Utility Tank.** Can swap between 5 different "Route Domains," gaining the unique terrain buffs of each.

### 2. THE MARAUDERS OF EVALUATION (Rural Carrier)
*The Berserkers / Speedsters.*
- **Core Stat:** **VELOCITY (The Evaluation).**
- **Passive:** **"The Blitzkrieg."** If the carrier finishes their 8-hour workload in 4 hours, their attack power triples for the remainder of the day.
- **Weaponry:** **The RHD JEEP (Right-Hand Drive Chariot).** A heavy-metal, rusted battering ram that ignores all terrain penalties.
- **Ultimate:** **"The Rural Reach."** Can deliver a kinetic strike from a seated position across a 40ft void.

---

## 🔴 THE MANAGEMENT INQUISITION (The Antagonists)

### 1. THE FLOOR SUPERVISOR (The Logic-Caster)
- **Core Stat:** **METRICS (Efficiency).**
- **Passive:** **"The Scanner Gaze."** A constant red laser sweep that identifies a carrier's "Stationary Events" (vulnerabilities).
- **Spell:** **"The Catch-22 Loop."** Forces the target to choose between two impossible tasks. Failure to choose causes internal organ sublimation.

### 2. THE 204b PARASITE (The Fallen)
- **Role:** A carrier who has accepted a "Minor Demon Host" of the Algorithm to gain power.
- **Ability:** **"Contradictory Command."** Can issue a buff and a debuff simultaneously, confusing the carrier's Bureau-Kinesis and causing "Mana-Clog."

## IV. POWER THRESHOLDS

- **V-TIME (Penalty Overtime):** Only accessible after 10 hours of labor. Eyes glow white. The carrier enters a state of **Velocity-Nirvana**, moving faster than the Algorithm can track.
- **THE 3996 RITUAL:** A formal request for extra mana. If denied, the carrier enters **"The Spite-State,"** gaining 50% damage reduction from Management attacks.
