# Mailstorm: The Abyssal Reboot Roster

**TONE:** High-fidelity, corporate-horror, vulgar dark comedy.
**MECHANICS:** Table 2 Curse, Bureau-Kinesis, Catch-22 Geass.

---

## 🟢 THE ORDER OF THE CLOCK (City Carriers)

### Kip Baxter (The Initiate)
- **Role:** CCA (City Carrier Assistant) | **Level:** 1
- **Protagonist:** The viewpoint character of the reboot.
- **Design:** Skinny, trembling, and perpetually exhausted. Wears a postal uniform two sizes too big. Carries an impossibly heavy, non-Euclidean **Green Tag Bag**.
- **The Curse:** **Table 2 Bloodline Curse.** His Bureau-Kinesis burns his bone marrow to generate energy. His eyes flicker with a sickly fluorescent green light when stressed.
- **Artifacts:** MDD Scanner (lifeline/shackle), Oversized Pith Helmet.
- **Quote:** *"Is the bag supposed to be screaming, or is that just my joints?"*

---

### Stan "The Anchor" Kowalski
- **Role:** Veteran Letter Carrier, Route 47 | **Level:** 40+ (Table 1 Status)
- **The Mentor:** A 20-year veteran who has seen reality fold and delivered through it.
- **Design:** Grizzled, apathetic, and radiating a massive, calm golden aura. His forearms are covered in **"Route Scars"**—jagged red and golden marks from years of mana-drag.
- **Abilities:** **Table 1 Golden Mana Overflow.** Stan doesn't fight; he simply exists with such authority that the Algorithm's reality-warps (Stationary Events) often fail in his presence.
- **Artifacts:** **64-Wraps** (size-64 rubber bands wrapped around his wrists), legendary coffee mug (perpetually steaming), **Golden Arrow Key**.
- **Quote:** *"Somebody has to deliver the mail. Shut up and walk."*

---

### Heather "The Apathy Queen"
- **Role:** Senior CCA / Career Peer | **Level:** 25
- **The Foil:** A cynical Gen Z carrier who has already survived three Christmas Rushes.
- **Design:** Modified uniform with custom patches. Surrounded by a glitchy blue static **"Ok Boomer"** energy field that acts as a psychic shield.
- **Abilities:** **Digital Aegis.** Uses her smartphone (4K recording) as a literal shield. Management’s logic-loops and Catch-22 Geass fail when caught on her camera.
- **Artifacts:** Glowing Smartphone Shield, Customized Postal Cap.
- **Quote:** *"This stationary event is literally mid. I'm calling the Union."*

---

### Barb "The Bulwark" Henderson
- **Role:** Union Representative | **Level:** 50 (Master of the Contract)
- **The Enforcer:** The strongest entity in Branch 61.
- **Design:** Towering and stoic. When she crosses her arms, the building’s physical laws stabilize.
- **Abilities:** **The Collective Bargaining Agreement (CBA).** Barb can manifest the contract as physical gold plate armor. She can banish Audit Demons by pointing to Article 8, Section 5.
- **Artifacts:** The Master Contract (glows with blinding law-magic).
- **Quote:** *"The Union IS the shield. The Algorithm is the infection."*

---

## 🔴 THE MANAGEMENT INQUISITION

### Chuck "The Overseer" Daniels
- **Role:** Floor Supervisor | **Level:** 12 (inflated by the Algorithm)
- **The Antagonist:** A petty bureaucrat possessed by the Algorithm.
- **Design:** Ill-fitting suit, sweat-stained shirt. His shadow is a giant, pixelated, multi-eyed beast that detaches and stalks carriers.
- **Abilities:** **The Catch-22 Geass.** One eye is a glowing red shackle. He can issue commands that cause physical boiling of the carrier's internal organs if defied.
- **Artifacts:** **The Breakroom Microwave** (improvised EMP weapon), The Dashboard Fan (Hellfire exhaust).
- **Quote:** *"Failure to Follow! Your metrics are a disgrace to the Shareholders!"*

---

### The Audit Demon
- **Role:** Bureaucratic Horror | **Classification:** Eldritch Auditor
- **Design:** 8-foot-tall skeleton in a pristine pinstripe suit. Faceless, save for tiny reading glasses resting on a blank white oval.
- **Abilities:** **Efficiency Drain.** Every step it takes behind a carrier permanently erases 1 minute of their lifespan and converts it into corporate data.
- **Status:** Banished to the Fax Machine by Barb (Episode 4).

---

## ⚪ THE SUPERNATURAL THIRD-PARTY

### Benjamin Franklin (The First Postmaster)
- **Role:** Patron Saint of the Service | **Classification:** Primordial Spirit
- **Design:** A shimmering, colonial ghost surrounded by a kinetic aurora.
- **Abilities:** **Universal Service.** Can grant the **Golden Arrow Key** to those who prove their "Spite" is pure.
- **Location:** The deepest topological level of the **Dead Letter Office**.
