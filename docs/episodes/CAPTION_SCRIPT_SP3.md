# MAILSTORM — SPECIAL EPISODE 3: THE POSTMASTER'S GALA

### [PANEL 1: The Ascent]
* **Visual Direction:** The horrific exterior of the uppermost penthouse of the Brutalist Postal Ziggurat. It glows with sickly gold and deep crimson light through massive vertical concrete slats. The storm from the lower levels cannot touch this height. Dark corporate fantasy. 
* **Caption:** High above the frozen trenches, the air is perfectly climate-controlled.
* **Sub-caption:** THE UPPER MANAGEMENT STRATOSPHERE.

### [PANEL 2: The Ballroom]
* **Visual Direction:** Inside the Ziggurat penthouse. An opulent, Gatsby-esque corporate ballroom blended with 1970s brutalism. Red velvet carpets cover cold linoleum. High-society postal executives in mutated formalwear, wearing hard-hats decorated in gold leaf, mingling among brutalist pillars. 
* **Caption:** The District Managers gather to celebrate the quarterly metrics. 
* **Sub-caption:** DECADENCE FORGED FROM STOLEN WAGES.

### [PANEL 3: The Bishops]
* **Visual Direction:** The secretive Algorithm Bishops standing at the back of the room. They wear perfectly tailored 1970s postal inspector trench coats, but beneath their wide-brimmed hats, their faces are just glowing, swirling vortexes of green code and raw data.
* **Caption:** The Algorithm's clerics observe quietly. They do not drink. They only calculate. 
* **Sub-caption:** METRICS MADE MANIFEST.

### [PANEL 4: The Toast]
* **Visual Direction:** A massive, brutalist golden fountain in the center of the room flowing with a viscous, golden-black fluid. An executive with a grotesque smirk is sipping this fluid from a high-society cocktail glass that looks like it is made of crystallized, frozen paper forms. 
* **Caption:** The toast of the 'Distilled Undeliverables.' The liquefied life essence of crushed route evaluations.
* **Sub-caption:** SIP THE GRIEVANCE MANA. TASTE THE APATHY.

### [PANEL 5: The Contract Shredder]
* **Visual Direction:** The Postmaster General standing on a balcony. He is wearing a terrifying, impossibly intricate tailored suit stitched together entirely from shredded union contracts and grievance forms. He raises a golden-black glass over the balcony, looking down into the abyssal void below.
* **Caption:** The ritual completes. A thousand carrier evaluations are sacrificed to sustain their immortality. 
* **Sub-caption:** PRAY YOU NEVER RECEIVE AN INVITATION.
