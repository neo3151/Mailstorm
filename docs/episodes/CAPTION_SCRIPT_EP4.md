# Episode 4: THE CBA SHIELD
**Arc:** Kip is cornered by an OIG Inquisitor over the contraband DLO envelope and must invoke the Weingarten Summon to manifest his first defensive magic, discovering the hidden Union Hall Cathedral.
**New Characters:** The OIG Inquisitor, The Spectral NALC Steward.
**Lore Mechanics:** Step 1 Interrogation Room, Weingarten Rights, The CBA Shield, Grievance Spite.

## SCENE 1: THE SNARE — "THE RED SCREEN"
**Location:** INT. The Ziggurat (Time-Clock Vestibule) - Evening
**Tone:** Sudden Terror

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> A tight close-up on Kip’s MDD Scanner. The screen is glowing a violent, blinding red, casting harsh crimson light onto Kip’s exhausted face. The screen reads `FATAL ERROR: CONTRABAND DETECTED`. Behind Kip, looming out of the shadows, is a horrifying, unnaturally tall entity in a crisp white shirt—THE INQUISITOR. Its face is obscured by a swirling vortex of red tape.

**DIALOGUE:**
- **THE INQUISITOR:** "Initiate Baxter. You failed to manifest the Black Envelope. The Audit has arrived."
- **KIP:** "I didn't steal it! The Pivot Dimension spit it out!"

## SCENE 2: THE INTERROGATION — "CATCH-22 GEASS"
**Location:** INT. The Step 1 Void (Abstract Interrogation Dimension)
**Tone:** Crushing, Claustrophobic

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> Kip is floating in a weightless, pitch-black void. Wrapping entirely around him are impossibly dense, glowing red ribbons of text (The Catch-22 Logic Loop). The text reads things like "WALK FASTER BUT DO NOT RUN" and "DELIVER IT ALL BUT RETURN BY FIVE." Kip is visibly choking, grasping at his own throat as the red text tightens like a python.

**DIALOGUE:**
- **THE INQUISITOR (O.S.):** "You are late. You are unsafe. Confess to the theft of the DLO logic and your termination will be painless."
- **KIP:** (Choking) "No... the rules..."

**CAPTION NOTE:** \[ARCHIVE MECHANIC: A Step 1 Interrogation strips the carrier's stamina. Without a shield, logic loops are fatal.\]

## SCENE 4: THE PAPER CATHEDRAL — "THE SHIELD MANIFESTS"
**Location:** INT. The Ethereal Union Hall
**Tone:** Awe-inspiring, Holy

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> A massive, gorgeous splash page. The dark void has shattered completely. We are now inside an impossibly vast, glowing Cathedral inside the Ziggurat. But instead of stone, the soaring pillars and arches are constructed entirely out of stacked, dense white paperwork (The Master Contract). Standing in front of Kip is a towering, spectral NALC Stewart—a ghostly, grizzled veteran carrier radiating a blinding white aura. The Steward is effortlessly holding back the Inquisitor with one hand.

**DIALOGUE:**
- **THE STEWARD:** "Step back, Management. You have no Just Cause here."
- **THE INQUISITOR:** (Recoiling) "The Shield... He invoked it."

**CAPTION NOTE:** \[LORE CODEX: The Union Hall is not a room. It is a bunker of grievances forged over sixty years of blood and ink.\]

## SCENE 5: THE WEAPON — "GRIEVANCE SPITE"
**Location:** INT. The Time-Clock Vestibule - Evening

But now, jagged, crackling red-hot electricity (Grievance Spite) is pooling heavily into the rubber bands, glowing menacingly in the dim fluorescent light.
