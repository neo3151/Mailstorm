# SP.01: ENLIST IN THE OIG (Propaganda Ad)
**Arc:** An invasive, corporate advertisement explaining the "benefits" of becoming a 204b Acting Supervisor, which rapidly devolves into a terrifying body-horror warning about the managerial parasite taking over carrier souls.
**Style:** 1950s Fallout Propaganda, Cheerful Atomic-Age Mascots decaying into Eldritch Horror.

---

## SCENE 1: THE PROMISE
**Location:** 1950s Ad Poster
**Tone:** Cheerful, Manipulative

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> A retro, 1950s atomic-age illustrated advertisement poster. A cheerful, classically drawn cartoon postal worker in a pristine blue uniform is walking through a bright, sunny neighborhood. He looks hopelessly exhausted, carrying a massive, towering stack of heavy Amazon boxes that are breaking his back. The art is clean, nostalgic, and brightly colored.

**CAPTION NOTE:** \[OIG RECRUITMENT\]: "ARE YOUR FEET TIRED? IS THE MAIL TOO HEAVY?"

---

## SCENE 2: THE PROMOTION
**Location:** 1950s Ad Poster
**Tone:** Aspirational Corporate

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> A retro, 1950s atomic-age advertisement. The same postal worker is now sitting behind a massive, clean wooden office desk. He is wearing a crisp white dress shirt with a red tie. He is cheerfully holding a white ceramic coffee mug that says "#1 SUPERVISOR." He is smiling brightly at the camera. The style is heavily stylized 1950s propaganda.

**CAPTION NOTE:** \[OIG RECRUITMENT\]: "STEP OFF THE STREET. BECOME A ✨204B ACTING SUPERVISOR✨!"

---

## SCENE 3: THE HOST BODY
**Location:** Glitching 1950s Poster
**Tone:** Unsettling, Glitching Hardware

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> The retro 1950s style starts to heavily glitch and corrupt with digital VHS static and dark shadows. The cheerful postal worker at the desk is sweating profusely. A massive, horrific, shadowy centipede-like creature with too many legs is crawling up his back, wrapping around his neck. His smile is now strained and terrified. Blood is dripping from his nose.

**CAPTION NOTE:** \[SYSTEM LOG\]: "PLEASE DO NOT RESIST THE PARASITE. IT CRAVES THE CLIPBOARD."

---

## SCENE 4: THE DIGESTION
**Location:** Full Body Horror
**Tone:** Eldritch Terror, Liminal

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> The 1950s aesthetic is completely gone, replaced by pure gritty, high-contrast chiaroscuro corporate horror. A closeup on the postal worker's horrific face. His eyes are rolled entirely back into his skull, emitting a blinding white light. A glowing, metallic device is shoved aggressively down his throat. The shadows around him are twisting into jagged teeth. Pure nightmare fuel.

**CAPTION NOTE:** \[OIG RECRUITMENT\]: "THE HOST BODY WILL BE FULLY DIGESTED TO MAKE ROOM FOR METRICS."

---

## SCENE 5: THE 204B PARASITE
**Location:** Corporate Hellscape
**Tone:** Bleak Brutalism

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> An absolutely terrifying, towering humanoid entity in a sharp black corporate suit standing in a dark, rusted warehouse. Where its head should be is a swirling, violent vortex of shredded paperwork, red tape, and glowing red barcode scanning lasers. It is holding a glowing golden clipboard. High contrast lighting. Truly horrific postal monstrosity.

**CAPTION NOTE:** \[OIG RECRUITMENT\]: "WELCOME TO MANAGEMENT."
