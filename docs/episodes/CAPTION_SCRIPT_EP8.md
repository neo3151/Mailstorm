# MAILSTORM — EPISODE 8: THE IRON LIFTERS

### [PANEL 1: The Prime Tsunami]
* **Visual Direction:** A catastrophic dimensional rift has torn open inside the cathedral-sized Bulk Mail Center. Pouring out of the rift is a 50-foot sentient wave made entirely of smiling brown Amazon boxes, packing tape, and heavy pet food boxes. It threatens to bury the brutalist sorting floor. The scale is apocalyptic.
* **Caption:** The Algorithm weaponizes volume. A localized breach on Dock 42 births the Prime Tsunami.
* **Sub-caption:** THE WAREHOUSE GROANS UNDER THE WEIGHT.

### [PANEL 2: The Joust]
* **Visual Direction:** A massive, heavily muscled Mail Handler (NPMHU) wearing industrial steel-toed boots and a sweat-stained union shirt charges head-first into the cardboard wave. He is riding and revving a 5,000lb hydraulic pallet jack like a mecha-lance jousting knight, smashing through the front line of boxes.
* **Caption:** A clerk sorts. A carrier walks. The Iron Lifters break the line. 
* **Sub-caption:** KINETIC FORCE IS THE ONLY CURRENCY HERE.

### [PANEL 3: Siege Bombardment]
* **Visual Direction:** A line of furious mail handlers on an elevated metal catwalk. Their veins are glowing bright orange with pure Spite Mana. They are hurling 70lb white mail sacks at hypersonic speeds down into the Amazon box horde. The sacks are visibly breaking the sound barrier with mach-cone shockwaves as they carpet-bomb the lower floor.
* **Caption:** The "Night Throw" isn't a job requirement. It's a localized orbital strike.
* **Sub-caption:** SOUND BARRIERS SHATTER ON THE NIGHT SHIFT.

### [PANEL 4: The Initial D Forklift Drift]
* **Visual Direction:** A massive, terrifyingly heavy 10,000lb yellow industrial forklift is literally drifting sideways at high speed through an aisle of the warehouse. The Tour 1 Forklift Master is at the wheel, eyes glowing, burning rubber as the back end of the forklift swings around to obliterate a towering wall of cursed Amazon packages. Extreme motion blur, anime-style action lines. 
* **Caption:** The Cavalry doesn't ride horses anymore. They run on propane and industrial rubber.
* **Sub-caption:** THE TOUR 1 DRIFT MASTER HAS ARRIVED.

### [PANEL 5: The Dock Slam]
* **Visual Direction:** The climax. Every Mail Handler on the floor synchronized together pulling the heavy iron chains of 40 consecutive garage-style loading dock doors. The massive steel doors crash down into the concrete floor simultaneously, creating a visible, glowing shockwave of pneumatic force that physically blasts the entire 50-foot Amazon wave back into the dimensional rift.
* **Caption:** The Iron Lifters sync their Bureau-Kinesis for the ultimate defensive ward. 
* **Sub-caption:** THE DOCK SLAM SEALS THE BREACH.
