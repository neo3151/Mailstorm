# MAILSTORM — EPISODE 9: THE ALL-CALL BLIZZARD

### [PANEL 1: The Freeze]
* **Visual Direction:** A punishing, unnatural blizzard of absolute zero freezing the exterior of a 1970s brutalist Post Office. Everything is encased in jagged, thick blue ice. The skies are dark and ominous. The aesthetic is grim, frozen corporate-horror.
* **Caption:** The Algorithm initiates a Level 10 Weather Event to force a system-wide failure.
* **Sub-caption:** INFINITE INFRACTIONS AT ABSOLUTE ZERO.

### [PANEL 2: Iron Lifter Breach]
* **Visual Direction:** Inside the frozen loading docks, a massive Mail Handler (NPMHU) swinging a heavy steel bar smashes through a 20-foot wall of glacial ice blocking the bay doors. Chunks of ice fly everywhere. 
* **Caption:** NPMHU Heavy Infantry strike first. The Dock Slam shatters the glacial blockade.
* **Sub-caption:** THE GATES OF THE ZIGGURAT ARE FORCED OPEN.

### [PANEL 3: The Clerk Furnace]
* **Visual Direction:** Deep inside the dark sorting facility, APWU clerks wearing thick gloves are physically overriding massive industrial DBCS sorting machines. The machines are spinning so fast they glow red-hot, acting as thermal furnaces to melt the ice encroaching on the floor.
* **Caption:** APWU Artificers over-volt the sorting leviathans to generate a localized thermal core.
* **Sub-caption:** BUREAUCRATIC FRICTION BECOMES HEAT.

### [PANEL 4: The Rural Convoy]
* **Visual Direction:** A convoy of heavily modified, armored mail carrier vehicles (Subarus and old Jeeps) equipped with massive snow-plow spikes smashing their way through a buried, frozen city street. It looks like a winter Mad Max. 
* **Caption:** The NRLCA Berserkers lock their vehicle wards into a snow-piercing assault line. 
* **Sub-caption:** THE FRONTIER CAVALRY BREAKS THE ROAD.

### [PANEL 5: The Paladin March]
* **Visual Direction:** NALC City Carriers wearing thick postal blue uniforms that glow strongly with bright orange "Spite Mana." They are marching determinedly out of the convoy paths into the raging whiteout storm, carrying glowing parcels. Their anger is keeping them warm. 
* **Caption:** NALC Paladins weaponize their Grievance Engine as an absolute thermal shield. 
* **Sub-caption:** FOUR FACTIONS UNITED. THE COVENANT DELIVERS.
