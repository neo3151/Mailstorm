# EPISODE 5: DEAD LETTER DEEP-DIVE
**Status:** Beat Outline and Lore Alignment

## 1. NARRATIVE GOAL
Introduce the APWU (The Clerks) and transition the story from physical survival on the streets to deep lore investigation inside the subterranean infrastructure of the post office.

## 2. NEW CHARACTERS / ENTITIES
- **The Undeliverables (DLO Swarm):** Grotesque amalgamations of torn cardboard, shattered porcelain shipments, and angry lost memories that wander the basement vaults like zombies.
- **Heather (The APWU Artificer):** A brutalist, cyberpunk clerk. She wears a faded blue vest adorned with arcane barcode patches. Attached to her forearm is a heavy, modified IMDAS laser scanner wired directly into her visor.

## 3. LORE MECHANICS
- **The DLO Catacombs:** An infinite, shifting maze of shelving units beneath the Ziggurat where mail with no destination rots.
- **Machine Communion:** The APWU passive ability to force order upon chaotic parcels by violently applying standard routing codes.
- **The IMDAS Override:** Using a barcode scanner to "un-encrypt" raw magical data embedded in physical paper.

## 4. THE 5-PANEL BEATS
**Beat 1 (The Descent):** Kip walks down a rusted, seemingly endless spiral staircase plunging into pitch black.
**Beat 2 (The Swarm):** At the bottom, Kip is ambushed by a towering monstrosity made entirely of crushing, unlabeled cardboard boxes (The Undeliverables).
**Beat 3 (The Flash):** Before Kip is crushed, a blinding green laser beam slices the monster in half. Heather steps out of the shadows holding a massive, weaponized handheld scanner.
**Beat 4 (The Override):** Heather takes Kip back to her sorting terminal. She plugs the Black DLO Letter into a mess of wires and slams her scanner into it.
**Beat 5 (The Coordinates):** A holographic green map composed of floating USPS tracking numbers projects above the letter. The screen blares that the item must be delivered to the Algorithm's core.
