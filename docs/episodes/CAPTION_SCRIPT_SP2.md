# SP.02: PRIME DAY MASSACRE (One-Shot Special)
**Arc:** A standalone grindhouse-horror vignette following a nameless Rural Carrier trapped in their LLV on Prime Day as the crushing weight of Amazon packages morphs into a sentient, violent swarm of cardboard golems.
**Style:** 1970s Grindhouse Horror Comic, high contrast film grain, intense brutal action, blinding reds and rusted yellows.

---

## SCENE 1: THE SWARM
**Location:** Rural Dirt Road (Exterior LLV)
**Tone:** B-Movie Horror Poster

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> A 1970s grindhouse horror comic style shot. A beat-up, rusted USPS Long-Life Vehicle (LLV) sits stranded on an incredibly dusty, barren dirt road during a blood-red sunset. The back door of the LLV is bursting open, and hundreds of jagged, crushed Amazon boxes are violently erupting out of it like a living, writhing swarm of insects. Heavy film grain, high-octane pulpy aesthetic. 

**CAPTION NOTE:** PRIME DAY DEBUFF ACTIVATED. THE VOLUME HAS BECOME SENTIENT.

---

## SCENE 2: THE BREACH
**Location:** Inside the LLV
**Tone:** Claustrophobic Terror

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> Inside the cramped, rusted cabin of the LLV. A massive, horrifying golem made entirely of jagged, ripped cardboard and packing tape is violently smashing its way through the sliding metal door separating the cab from the cargo bay. A nameless, terrified Rural Carrier in a bucket hat is desperately kicking the cardboard monster back with their boots. Deep shadows, visceral action lines.

**CAPTION NOTE:** NO SCAN FOR THIS STOP. SURVIVAL IS THE ONLY METRIC.

---

## SCENE 3: THE DOG SPRAY
**Location:** Inside the LLV
**Tone:** Explosive Retaliation

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> The Rural Carrier is standing their ground in the driver's seat. They are aggressively firing a standard postal Dog Repellent spray can at the cardboard golem, but instead of pepper spray, a massive, blinding torrent of liquid fire is erupting from the nozzle, engulfing the cardboard monster. High-contrast yellow and red flames light up the grim, sweaty face of the carrier. Pulp comic book violence. 

**CAPTION NOTE:** STANDARD ISSUE DEFENSIVE INCENDIARY DEPLOYED.

---

## SCENE 4: THE TUB SHIELD
**Location:** Inside the LLV
**Tone:** Brutal Melee

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> The burning cardboard monster is lashing out with a terrifying, jagged tape-covered claw. The Rural Carrier violently blocks the strike using a shattered white plastic US Mail tub strapped to their forearm like a gladiator's riot shield. Splinters of plastic and sparks are exploding outward from the brutal impact. The carrier's eyes are wide with adrenaline.

**CAPTION NOTE:** HEAVY BLUNT TRAUMA SUSTAINED. THE PARCEL MUST BE ENDED.

---

## SCENE 5: THE SURVIVOR
**Location:** Atop the Rusted LLV
**Tone:** Triumphant Grindhouse Finale

**PANEL DIRECTION:**
> The rural carrier, covered in sweat, dirt, and shredded cardboard, is standing victoriously on the dented aluminum roof of their LLV. They are holding up a torn Amazon prime-tape ribbon like a bloody trophy. The sky behind them is a violent, saturated crimson. A classic 1970s horror comic victory pose. Pure, unapologetic pulp exploitation aesthetic.

**CAPTION NOTE:** THE ROUTE REMAINS OPEN. THE ENDLESS CARNAGE CONTINUES TUESDAY.
