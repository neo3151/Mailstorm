# MAILSTORM — EPISODE 10: THE SOLIDARITY MANDATE

### [PANEL 1: The Avatar]
* **Visual Direction:** The frozen concrete sorting floor. In the center, a towering, horrific biomechanical entity constructed of glowing green scanner glass, brutalist concrete chunks, and jagged metal wire. The physical manifestation of The Algorithm. 
* **Caption:** The Algorithm descends. The localized infrastructure warps around its physical mass.
* **Sub-caption:** INFINITE METRICS. ZERO EMPATHY.

### [PANEL 2: The Four Guilds Assemble]
* **Visual Direction:** Surrounding the towering Avatar are the four united postal factions. A NALC Paladin glowing with Spite Mana, a massive NPMHU Mail Handler with a steel pallet jack, an APWU Artificer crackling with electrical heat, and a NRLCA Berserker standing on the hood of a spiked LLV. 
* **Caption:** Forty years of division erased in a single moment. 
* **Sub-caption:** THE TRIBES OF THE ZIGGURAT STAND TOGETHER.

### [PANEL 3: The Invocation]
* **Visual Direction:** Kip, in the center of the factions, his blue postal uniform tearing at the seams with blinding, raw orange and blue magical energy. He is holding up a heavy, glowing bureaucratic tome.
* **Caption:** Kip channels the combined grievance mana of the entire workforce. 
* **Sub-caption:** HE INVOKES THE MASTER CONTRACT.

### [PANEL 4: The Solidarity Mandate]
* **Visual Direction:** A devastating, kinetic explosion of pure 1970s Liminal Brutalist magic. A shockwave of hazard-orange and electric-blue energy obliterating the concrete Avatar. The kinetic force shatters the surrounding frozen ice. Epic anime-style annihilation.
* **Caption:** The Solidarity Mandate strikes the core. The Algorithm is temporarily severed.
* **Sub-caption:** THE UNION BREAKS THE MACHINE.

### [PANEL 5: The Aftermath]
* **Visual Direction:** The dust clears. The Avatar is destroyed, but standing in the shadows of the destroyed loading dock is a slick, perfectly clean figure in a tailored black suit, holding a glowing golden disciplinary clipboard. The OIG has arrived. 
* **Caption:** The facility breathes. But the victory triggers an automated escalation. 
* **Sub-caption:** SEASON TWO CONTINUES THE STORM.
