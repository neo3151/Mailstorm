# EPISODE 1: "The Shadow Day" - Production Sheet
**REMASTER:** Abyssal Standard Edition.
**VISUAL STYLE:** 80/20 Chiaroscuro | 3.5 Panel Density.

---

## 📑 PAGE 1: THE THRESHOLD (The Monolith)
**GOAL:** Establish the sheer, cold scale of the Abyss.

- **PANEL 1 (Wide):**
    - **Scene:** EXT. BRANCH 61 ZIGGURAT.
    - **Composition:** Extreme low angle looking up at the windowless concrete walls. Heavy diesel smog in the air.
    - **Chiaroscuro:** 90% shadow. Only the hard edges of the concrete are defined by a cold, morning rim-light.
    - **Dialogue:** (Caption) *Branch 61: The Tomb of Efficiency.*

- **PANEL 2 (Close-Up):**
    - **Scene:** KIP'S HANDS.
    - **Composition:** Focused on Kip's trembling, bony hands gripping the heavy iron handle. The oversized uniform sleeve hangs low.
    - **SFX:** (Integrated) *TREMBLE... TREMBLE...*

- **PANEL 3 (Action):**
    - **Scene:** THE DOORS OPEN.
    - **Composition:** A blast of white, sickly fluorescent light from the interior spills out, silhouetting Kip.
    - **SFX:** (Jagged) **GROOOOAN.**

---

## 📑 PAGE 2: THE AWAKENING (Shadow Severance)
**GOAL:** High-intensity ritual and body horror.

- **PANEL 1 (Wide/Interior):**
    - **Scene:** THE PIT.
    - **Composition:** Non-Euclidean depth. Sorting cases stretch into infinity. Fluorescent lights flickering in 4000K yellow.
    - **Chiaroscuro:** 70% shadow. The floor is a grid of deep blacks.

- **PANEL 2 (Mid):**
    - **Scene:** STAN'S AURA.
    - **Composition:** Stan standing calm. A heavy, golden "Golden Grievance Mana" aura vibrates around his 64-Wraps.
    - **Dialogue:** Stan: "Don't say the name. Just stand still."

- **PANEL 3 (Action):**
    - **Scene:** THE STRIKE.
    - **Composition:** Stan’s boot slams into the concrete, pinning Kip's SHADOW.
    - **SFX:** (Deep) **THUD.**

- **PANEL 4 (Close-Up/Horror):**
    - **Scene:** THE SEVERANCE.
    - **Composition:** Kip’s face mid-scream. The shadow is thrashing and bleeding black ink from under the boot.
    - **Chiaroscuro:** Extreme contrast. Kip's eyes are pure, hollow white.

---

## 📑 PAGE 3: THE ALGORITHM'S GAZE (The Crush)
**GOAL:** Psychological terror and environmental danger.

- **PANEL 1 (Wide):**
    - **Scene:** CHUCK ON THE CRATE.
    - **Composition:** Chuck is a silhouette against the flickering lights. His shadow is a multi-eyed monster towering above the group.
    - **Dialogue:** Chuck: "THE METRICS ARE DOWN! THE ALGORITHM IS HUNGRY!"

- **PANEL 2 (Mid):**
    - **Scene:** THE MDD ALARM.
    - **Composition:** The purple screen of the MDD screams "STATIONARY EVENT."
    - **SFX:** (Electronic) **BEEP-BEEP-BEEP!**

- **PANEL 3 (Action):**
    - **Scene:** THE CRUSH.
    - **Composition:** Kip is slammed into the linoleum. We see his postal badge cracking under invisible pressure.
    - **Dialogue:** Kip: "I... can’t... breathe... The Table 2 Curse..."

- **PANEL 4 (Mid):**
    - **Scene:** NIXIE EMERGENCE.
    - **Composition:** A bubbling tub of UBBM sludge. A mangled grey phantom made of torn magazines claws its way out.
    - **SFX:** (Slithering) **SQUELCH.**

---

## 📑 PAGE 4: THE ANCHOR STRIKE (Climax & Resolution)
**GOAL:** Pure "WOW" power and cynicism.

- **PANEL 1 (Mid):**
    - **Scene:** STAN RAISING HIS ARM.
    - **Composition:** Stan pulling back a glowing size-64 rubber band. Pure **Golden Grievance Mana** illuminates his grizzly face.
    - **Dialogue:** Stan: "Chuck. 1571 Absolution denied."

- **PANEL 2 (SPLASH PANEL - Full Page Rank):**
    - **Scene:** THE VAPORIZATION.
    - **Composition:** The rubber band snapping through the Nixie Phantom. A massive sonic cone (white) shatters the 80% black background.
    - **SFX:** (Large/Concrete Cracking) **CRACK-BOOM!**

- **PANEL 3 (Mid):**
    - **Scene:** THE AFTERMATH.
    - **Composition:** Stan walking away in deep shadow. He drops a single rubber band onto Kip's bruised chest.
    - **Dialogue:** Stan: "Get up, Initiate. You've got five minutes. Welcome to the Abyss."
