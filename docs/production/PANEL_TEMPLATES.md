# THE ABYSSAL MANA: Master Panel Templates
**STYLE:** Heavy Chiaroscuro (80/20 Shadow-to-Light ratio).
**FORMAT:** Traditional Manga Grid (High-Impact / Non-Sequential).

---

## 🎨 I. VISUAL ARCHITECTURE
The "Abyssal Standard" requires a visceral, high-fidelity look. Every panel must feel like it was carved from concrete.

### 1. The Chiaroscuro Ratio (80 Black : 20 White)
- **Backgrounds:** Keep in deep shadow. Only define the edges of Brutalist pillars with harsh, thin white rim lights.
- **Characters:** Use heavy, chunky blacks for hair and clothing (uniforms). Only highlight faces and eyes (Glowing Mana).
- **Textures:** Use "Raw Concrete" noise and "Diesel Grime" gradients to bridge the gaps between pure black and white.

### 2. The Gutter Logic (Traditional Manga)
- **Standard Gap:** **0.25 inches (6mm).**
- **Action Impact:** Panels should overlap or tilt (Dutch angles) during combat scenes.
- **Breaking the Frame:** Energy effects (Grievance Mana), blood, or "Nixie" claws MUST bleed over the panel borders to simulate "Metric-Overflow."

---

## 📐 II. PANEL GEOMETRY

### Type A: The Monolith (Full Height/Width)
- **Use for:** Establishing shots of Branch 61 or a character's "Aura Awakening."
- **Design:** No borders on the sides. The background should bleed into the page edges.

### Type B: The Rapid-Fire (Small Grids)
- **Use for:** The **18/8 Hand-Seal Jutsu** (Sorting mail) or fast dialogue.
- **Design:** Jagged, straight-edged borders. No rounded corners.

### Type C: The Impact Beat (Large Inset)
- **Use for:** The Climax. (e.g., Stan’s size-64 rubber band snap).
- **Design:** The central panel should have a 4pt thick black border, with the action "shattering" the frame.

---

## 🖋️ III. SFX & LETTERING
- **Sound Effects:** Hand-lettered, integrated into the environment. (e.g., the **"CRACK-BOOM!"** should look like it's cracking the concrete walls).
- **Speech Bubbles:** 
    - **Normal:** Standard oval with a 2pt stroke.
    - **The Algorithm:** Rectangular, pixelated borders, with font colors inverted (Primary green on black).
    - **Grievance:** Jagged, vibrating borders (Red/Black).
