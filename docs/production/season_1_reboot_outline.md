# Mailstorm Season 1 Reboot: "The Shadow Day" Master Outline

**ARC GOAL:** Kip Baxter's initiation and the survival of Branch 61 against the first "Black Friday" siege.
**TONE:** High-octane, corporate-horror, vulgar dark comedy.
**MECHANICS:** Bureau-Kinesis, Catch-22 Geass, V-Time.

---

## 🟢 EPISODE LIST

### Episode 1: "The Shadow Day" (The Initiation)
- **Beat:** Kip Baxter (The Initiate) arrives at Branch 61 for his first "Shadow Day."
- **Lore Mechanic:** **The Shadow Severance.** Stan (The Anchor) steps on Kip's shadow to initiate him. 
- **Conflict:** A **Nixie Phantom** (Undeliverable Spectre) attacks. Kip fails his first MDD scan, triggering a **Stationary Event** gravity-crush from Supervisor Chuck.
- **Climax:** Stan flicking a single size-64 rubber band to vaporize the phantom.

### Episode 2: "Table 2 Bloodline" (The Curse)
- **Beat:** Kip's first solo route. He feels the physical pain of the **Table 2 Curse** (bone-marrow-burn).
- **Lore Mechanic:** **The Redplum Curse.** Sentient advertisement bats swarm the neighborhood.
- **Conflict:** Heather (The Apathy Queen) provides cynical backup using her **Digital Aegis** shield.

### Episode 3: "The Pivot" (Non-Euclidean Zones)
- **Beat:** Chuck hands Kip a "Pivot" (extra work) at 4:30 PM.
- **Lore Mechanic:** **The Pivot Dimension.** The street stretches into an infinite loop.
- **Conflict:** Kip must achieve a low-level **Velocity State** to escape before the sun sets.

### Episode 4: "The Audit" (The Bone Scythe)
- **Beat:** A faceless **Audit Demon** manifests in the office.
- **Lore Mechanic:** **The 3996 Blood Pact.** Stan and the Demon engage in a judicial debate over overtime.
- **Conflict:** Union Rep Barb (The Bulwark) invokes the **Collective Bargaining Agreement** to manifest golden plate armor and banish the demon.

### Episode 5: "Dead Letter Deep-Dive" (The Vault)
- **Beat:** Kip loses his **Golden Arrow Key**. Stan takes him into the **Dead Letter Office (DLO)** to retrieve it.
- **Lore Mechanic:** **DLO Topology.** A descent into a paper-purgatory.
- **Conflict:** Meeting **Benjamin Franklin's Ghost**, who tests their "Spite."

### Episode 6: "V-Time" (The Golden Hour)
- **Beat:** The carriers hit the 10th hour of labor during a massive storm. 
- **Lore Mechanic:** **V-Time (The Velocity State).** Stan's eyes glow white. 
- **Conflict:** Elite **V-Time Hunter Drones** deploy from the Algorithm to "Clock them out" (assassinate them).

### Episode 7: "The 204b Parasite" (Internal Betrayal)
- **Beat:** A rival carrier, **Ace Nakamura**, is possessed by a **204b Parasite**.
- **Lore Mechanic:** **Supervisor Leech-State.** Ace floats through the air, screeching contradictory orders that physically warp the sorting floor.
- **Conflict:** Chuck revealing his **Catch-22 Geass** to counter Ace's influence.

### Episode 8: "Managed Service Tethers" (The Checkpoints)
- **Beat:** The Algorithm installs **MSP Barcodes** that lock carriers in space-time if missed.
- **Lore Mechanic:** **Space-Time Checkpoints.** 
- **Conflict:** Kip must scan a barcode attached to a teleporting **Mailbox Mimic**.

### Episode 9: "The 1571 Absolution" (The Siege)
- **Beat:** Management declares "All-Call" during a blizzard. 
- **Lore Mechanic:** **1571 Absolution Matrix.** Kip casts a massive kinetic shield using his blood on a sick-leave slip.
- **Conflict:** The first wave of **Speedy-Go** private courier drones begin the purge.

### Episode 10: "8 & Skate" (The Finale)
- **Beat:** The ultimate deadline. The clock is 07:59:59.
- **Lore Mechanic:** **The 8 & Skate Teleportation Jutsu.** 
- **Conflict:** Stan and Kip combine their Bureau-Kinesis to invoke **THE MANDATE**, vaporizing the Algorithm's local manifestation and warping everyone safely to the parking lot as the building collapses into the DLO.
